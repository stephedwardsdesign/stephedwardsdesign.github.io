ABOUT ME

Hello! I’m Steph, a graphic designer, Photographer and Illustrator from Sydney, Australia

I am forever being amazed by the possibilities of design and one of my greatest joys is being able to see a person’s idea or vision transform into reality. Design really has the power to change the world.


When I’m not busy working you’ll find me taking photos of this amazing world we live in and planning my next adventure. I am a lover of bad puns and bingeing good TV shows, and I have been known to converse solely in movie quotes. I am also a lover of design. My true love is print design but dabble in photography, illustration, hand crafted designs and typography. This is a collection of some of my most favourite design projects over the last few years, ranging from commercial to university to self initiated love projects.


My desire is to work in a fast pace environment where I am challenged to grow and develop as a designer within the industry. If you have any questions please feel free to contact me – I look forwards to working with you.
